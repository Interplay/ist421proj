Project Description

Within this integration project, our group was consulted by the Shipping Parcel Union (SPU), a shipping company based in Middletown, PA to integrate a process that makes it easier for order data to be organized for cross-referencing purposes. With many orders being added to the SPU's database each day along with the employee view being very difficult to read, our company as implemented an application process that remedies this.
